AMAPRO ENABLES EVERY AMPUTEE

Millions of poor people around the world are in need of a prosthesis. We dramatically reduce costs through 3D-printing assisted methods.

Why?

For millions of people in low-income countries, prosthesis are not affordable. They suffer from social exclusion and reduced quality-of-life. We aim make their life more liveable by employing 3D-printing technology and let local partners sell them at low prices.

How?

After an extensive period of design and research, this is our latest iteration.

Affordable

3D printers and PLA filament have become increasingly low-price. This directly translates to a decreased selling price.

Robust

Up to 10kg of downward force.
